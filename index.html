Before You Book

Frequent Questions

What documentation do I need to bring? +
Bring a valid Passport or National ID (DNI) and a credit card. A refundable deposit of up to €500 per jet ski may be charged at check-in. This is returned in full at the end of your session if there's no damage.
What is the minimum age? +
Riders must be 18+ to drive a jet ski, or 16+ with written parental consent. Passengers must be at least 10 years old (ages 8–10 are at Sotojets' discretion).
How many people fit on one jet ski? +
Maximum 2 people per jet ski, with a combined weight limit of 130 kg. Pricing is per jet ski — not per person — making it great value for couples or friends sharing a ride.
When should I arrive at the marina? +
Arrive at Sotogrande Marina at least 20 minutes before your scheduled start time. Latecomers will be moved to the next available slot, so plan ahead!
Do I need previous jet ski experience? +
Zero experience required. Our certified instructors give you a full safety and technique briefing before you set off, and they stay close by throughout your session. Absolute beginners are very welcome.
What happens if I cause damage? +
The deposit (up to €500) covers repair costs from accidental damage. If damage exceeds this amount, the rider is responsible for the balance. Riders who behave irresponsibly will be removed from the water immediately with no refund.
